The Rickhouse

Downtown Durham
Durham, North Carolina

The Rickhouse is an industrial-chic event space in the heart of downtown Durham that overlooks the famed Durham Athletic Park, original home of the Durham Bulls. Once a marketplace where tobacco vendors sold to cigarette companies, it remains a piece of Durham history and a reminder of the industry that helped shape the economy of Durham and North Carolina. With exposed brick, reclaimed wood accents, and warm, intimate lighting, it offers a relaxed yet elegant setting that feels like home — perfect for celebrating with family and friends.

Getting There

Parking at The Rickhouse is limited. We recommend using nearby public parking decks or rideshare services when possible.
