Black Tie Creative

Formal, Elegant, and a Little Unexpected

Creative black tie means starting with a polished formal foundation, then adding personality through bold color, rich texture, metallic details, or fashion-forward accessories.

Women's Inspiration

Men's Inspiration

✦

Gentlemen

  • Jackets: Try velvet, pattern, or rich color like forest green, burgundy, or midnight blue.
  • Trousers: Keep the base formal with black tuxedo trousers or sharply tailored dark slacks.
  • Accessories: Add a jewel-toned bow tie, silk pocket square, ascot, or polished statement detail.
✦

Ladies

  • Dresses: Gowns or elevated cocktail dresses in jewel tones, sequins, velvet, or metallics.
  • Alternatives: Chic tuxedo-style pantsuits, sleek skirts, or embellished separates are welcome.
  • Accessories: Finish with statement jewelry, metallic clutches, or bold dressy heels.
