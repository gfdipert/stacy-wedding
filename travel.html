Getting Around

If you're staying downtown, we recommend walking or using Uber or Lyft to get to and from the reception. Parking at The Rickhouse is limited, and rideshare is often the easiest option for out-of-town guests.

A rental car is not necessary for guests staying downtown, though it may be helpful if you plan to explore the Triangle or stay outside Durham.

Airport

Most guests flying in should use Raleigh-Durham International Airport (RDU), approximately 20 minutes from downtown Durham.
